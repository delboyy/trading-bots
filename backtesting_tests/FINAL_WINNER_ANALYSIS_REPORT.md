## üéØ STRATEGY BREAKDOWN & LOGIC

### **TIME-BASED SCALPING** (Top Performer)
**Logic:** Session-aware momentum trading
- **NY AM (9:30-11:30 ET):** Focus on opening momentum continuation
- **NY PM (14:00-16:00 ET):** Profit-taking patterns and reversals
- **London (03:00-11:30 ET):** Opening range breakouts
- **Entry:** Momentum + Volume spikes within session windows
- **Exit:** Time-based (12 bars) or profit targets (1%)

### **CANDLESTICK SCALPING** (Most Accurate)
**Logic:** Pattern recognition with volume confirmation
- **Patterns:** Hammer, Shooting Star, Engulfing, Morning/Evening Star
- **Confirmation:** Volume above average + trend alignment
- **Entry:** Pattern formation + volume spike
- **Exit:** Pattern completion or time-based limit

### **RSI SCALPING** (Most Trades)
**Logic:** Oscillator-based mean reversion
- **Oversold (<30):** Buy signals with volume
- **Overbought (>70):** Sell signals with volume
- **Confirmation:** Price near Bollinger Bands edges
- **Exit:** RSI normalization or profit targets

### **VOLUME BREAKOUT** (Highest Win Rate)
**Logic:** Price breakouts on volume spikes
- **Breakout Detection:** High/Low breaks recent ranges
- **Volume Confirmation:** 1.5x average volume minimum
- **Neutral RSI:** Avoid extreme conditions (35-65 RSI)
- **Exit:** Quick profits or time-based stops
